# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

We are committed to providing a welcoming and inspiring community for all. We expect all members of the DogWalker community to abide by this Code of Conduct. This includes contributors, maintainers, users, and anyone participating in discussions or activities related to the project.

## Our Standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to creating a positive environment include:

- **Being respectful** - Treat all community members with courtesy and respect
- **Being constructive** - Provide helpful feedback and contribute positively to discussions
- **Being inclusive** - Welcome and support people of all backgrounds and identities
- **Being professional** - Maintain a professional tone in all communications
- **Being patient** - Understand that people have different perspectives and experiences
- **Being collaborative** - Work together to solve problems and improve the project

## Unacceptable Behavior

Examples of unacceptable behavior include:

- **Harassment** - Offensive comments, insults, or personal attacks based on any protected characteristic (race, gender, religion, sexual orientation, disability, age, etc.)
- **Discrimination** - Treating people unfairly based on their identity or characteristics
- **Bullying** - Deliberate intimidation, mockery, or exclusion of others
- **Abuse** - Threats, violence, or hostile behavior toward anyone
- **Disrespect** - Dismissing, mocking, or belittling others' contributions or perspectives
- **Spam** - Promotional content, excessive off-topic messages, or repetitive posts
- **Privacy violations** - Sharing private information without consent

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to all spaces managed by the DogWalker project, including:

- GitHub repositories and discussions
- Pull request and issue comments
- Project documentation and wikis
- Official project communication channels
- Public events and conferences where the project is represented
- Any other community spaces organized by project maintainers

## Enforcement

Community members who violate this Code of Conduct may face consequences determined at the discretion of the project maintainers, including:

- A warning or request to cease the behavior
- Temporary or permanent ban from the community
- Removal of comments or contributions
- Reporting to appropriate authorities (if necessary)

## Reporting Violations

If you experience or witness behavior that violates this Code of Conduct, please report it by contacting the project maintainers at:

- Opening a private issue (if available)
- Sending an email to the project maintainers
- Contacting a community moderator

All reports will be treated confidentially and investigated promptly.
